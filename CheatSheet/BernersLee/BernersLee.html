Ferranti Mark 1. Thus, he developed an interest into computers early on.

Education

He attended Sheen Mont Primary School, and then went on to attend south-west London's Emanuel School from 1969 to
1973. He studied at The Queens's Collage, Oxford from 1973 to 1976, where he received a first-class Bachelor of Arts degree
in physics. While at university, Beners-Lee made a computer out of an old television set, wich he bought from a repair shop

The Internet

In 1984, he returned to CERN as a fellow. In 1989, CERN was the largest Internet node in Europe and Berners-Lee saw an
opportunity to join hypertext with the Internet- 'I just had to take the hypertext idea and connect it to the TCP and DNA ideas
and ta-da!-the World Wide Web '. In 1990, he created not only the first wb browser but also an editor and first web
server. The first website, an explanation of the WWW concept, was published on 1990-12-20 - avalable to the Internet via the
CERN network.
